Introducing the Micro Eco-Industrial Park: A Revolutionary System for Converting Agricultural Waste into Valuable Renewable Resources

The Micro Eco-Industrial Park is an innovative initiative by Satech S.A.S. Our facility converts avocado seeds and peels
into biochar, biofuels and electricity, contributing to a cleaner environment and a more sustainable economy.

By diverting tons of waste from landfills each year, we significantly reduce methane emissions and create valuable
resources.
